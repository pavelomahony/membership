Why Membership?

The Local is made by and for internationals living abroad. We are our audience.And we need your support.

  • Once upon a time

    In 2004, a newsletter sent to 12 people in a language class became the first edition of The Local. .

  • Fast forward 15 years

    The Local now has 5.5 million monthly readers across nine country sites.

  • Most importantly

    Our community includes more than 10,000 paying Members.

  • The reality

    As internet giants gobble up ad revenue, this financial support is crucial to our survival.

Why Membership?

We believe in connecting professionals and expats who lead international lives to the world around them. Our journalism focuses on enhancing their lives through relevant content, products and services, like yours.

What you get

Whether you’re in the business of expat services (eg. insurance, mortgages or tax), promoting jobs to English speakers, or you’re a consumer brand or otherwise looking to reach an international, expat audience, our platform is the right place for you. Here you can place banner ads, sponsored content and more.

How to join

Our expat audience is unlike any other globally. To reach them, the most successful approach, particularly in the ‘content is king’ era, is Native Advertising.

Who reads The Local?

Our readers are highly educated

92% of our readers have at least a college/university degree. 43% have a Masters degree or equivalent.

They are increasingly engaged

More than 60,000 people have signed up for our newsletters and over 10,000 of our readers have become paying Members of The Local in just over a year.

The Local's readers wield influence

27% are senior decision-makers. 24% have an annual income of €75,000 or higher. 58% work in organisations with over 100 employees.

They know where to go for news

72% trust The Local as their primary news source.
